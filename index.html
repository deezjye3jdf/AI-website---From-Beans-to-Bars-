From Bean to Bar

The Delicious Journey of Chocolate

Growing Cacao

Cacao trees thrive in tropical climates. Their pods contain the precious beans that become chocolate.

Fermentation & Drying

Fresh beans are fermented for days, then dried under the sun to develop deep chocolate flavor.

Roasting & Grinding

Roasting enhances aroma. Beans are ground into cocoa mass, the base of all chocolate.

Tempering & Molding

Chocolate is tempered for shine and snap, then molded into bars we know and love.
